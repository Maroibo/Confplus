About us

ConfPlus is a powerful online platform for organizing and managing conferences of all sizes.Our mission is to simplify the entire conference management process, from registration to post-event analysis, for both organizers and attendees. With our user-friendly interface and robust set of features, ConfPlus makes it easy for conference organizers to plan, promote, and execute successful events.
